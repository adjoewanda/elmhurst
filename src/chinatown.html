Chinatown

Elmhurst is growing rapidly to become a little Chinatown in Queens, in addition to Flushing and Chinatown in the City. The Elmhurst Chinatown is situated on Broadway. Previously a small area with Chinese shops, this newly evolved Chinatown has expanded with Southeast Asian businesses and shops in the area, including Malaysian, Indonesian, Thai, and Vietnamese.
